Pizza Recipe

Crispy, cooked to perfrection, homemade!

This recipe is easy to make, without many ingredients.

Check the list below for the required ingredients

Ingredients:

  • 500g of Flower
  • 2 Eggs
  • 100mlof milk
  • 250gof cheese
  • Tomato sauce

Steps

  1. Mix flour with eggs in a bowl of your choice
  2. Roll until the consistency is equal use more flour if needed
  3. stretch out the dough
  4. Add sauce to the base
  5. Sprinkle your cheese on top
  6. Bake for 15minutes on the middle shelf of oven at 200degrees
