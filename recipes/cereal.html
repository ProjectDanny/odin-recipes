Cereal Bar Recipe

Crispy, homemade!

This recipe is easy to make, without many ingredients.

Check the list below for the required ingredients

Ingredients:

  • 300g of cereal
  • 2 bars of chocolate
  • 50mlof honey

Steps

  1. Melt choclate
  2. Mix with cereal
  3. section and shape into bars
  4. Add honey on top
